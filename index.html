Privacy Policy

Movie Book Club · Effective February 19, 2026

What Movie Book Club Is

Movie Book Club is a mobile app for small groups to experience and discuss movies, books, TV shows, and other media together. It is designed to be calm and private — no social feeds, no algorithms, no ads.

Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Email address — used for account authentication
  • Username — chosen by you, visible to your club members and friends
  • Discussion posts and quotes — content you write in club discussions
  • Private notes — personal notes you write about works; visible only to you
  • Reading/watch progress — your progress through works and sections
  • Friendships — mutual friend connections you initiate and accept
  • Push notification tokens — used to deliver notifications to your device
  • Notification preferences — your chosen reminder timing settings

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the app's features:

  • Authenticating your account
  • Displaying your username to club members and friends
  • Showing your discussion posts to other club members
  • Tracking your progress through works and sections
  • Enforcing spoiler gates based on your progress and deadlines
  • Sending push notifications for deadline reminders and club activity
  • Showing your completed works to accepted friends (unless you mark them private)

Third-Party Services

The app relies on two third-party services:

  • Supabase — provides authentication and database hosting. Your data is stored in a Supabase-managed PostgreSQL database. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Expo — provides push notification delivery. Your device's push token is sent to Expo's notification service. Expo Privacy Policy

We do not use analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or any other third-party tools.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Your data is only visible to:

  • You (all your own data)
  • Your club members (discussion posts, username, progress within shared clubs)
  • Your accepted friends (username and completed works you haven't marked private)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security policies that restrict access at the database level. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent — there is no grace period or recovery option.

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile, memberships, progress, private notes, friendships, push tokens, and notification preferences are permanently deleted
  • Your discussion posts and suggestions are preserved for other club members, but your name is replaced with "Deleted User"
  • If you own a club, ownership is transferred to the next longest-tenured member

Children

Movie Book Club is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can reach us at moviebookclub.app@gmail.com.
